Cookies Policy

At SpotCars, we use cookies to enhance your user experience and to ensure the proper functioning of the site. This Cookies Policy explains what cookies are, what cookies we use, and how you can manage cookies on your device.

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device (computer, tablet, smartphone) by the websites you visit. They help websites remember information about your visit, such as preferences and settings, which makes future visits easier and more efficient.

What Cookies Do We Use?

  • Essential Cookies: These cookies are necessary for the proper functioning of the site and include session cookies that remember information during your visit.
  • Functional Cookies: These cookies help remember your preferences and settings, so you don’t have to re-enter them on subsequent visits.
  • Analytical Cookies: We use these cookies to collect information about how you use our site so we can improve our services and understand which pages you visit most often.
